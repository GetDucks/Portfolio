  • Created validation queries to support production readiness checks

Reporting

Developed and supported Snowflake reporting views using SAP order, delivery, purchasing, and shipment data. The work focused on transforming complex operational data into clear reporting outputs for business teams.

  • Reduced manual investigation by making issues easier to detect
